About Me

Bob Kane was an American comic book artist and writer best known for co-creating Batman. Born in 1915, Kane was inspired by silent film, pulp heroes, and artist Alex Raymond. He had a passion for drawing from a young age and went on to work for DC Comics. Besides comics, he enjoyed painting and spending time with his wife Elizabeth Sanders. His bold imagination helped bring to life one of the most enduring superheroes in history.
